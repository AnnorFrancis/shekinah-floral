The demo currently uses Unsplash placeholder URLs directly in the HTML.
To switch to real photos:
  1. Save images here (webp or jpg, ~1200px wide is plenty)
  2. Replace the https://images.unsplash.com/... URLs in the HTML files
     with ./assets/images/your-photo.webp (or ../assets/images/... from /admin)
