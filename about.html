Our Story

It started withOne Borrowed Table

Shekinah Floral Decor began in Dome with a single hall, a borrowed table and an unreasonable amount of love for flowers. The first bride cried when she saw her reception — and we have been chasing that moment ever since.

Today we are a full-service decoration house: floral design, balloon artistry, venue transformation and rentals, serving families and businesses across Greater Accra and beyond. Three phone lines, one promise — we show up, we deliver, and it will be beautiful.

The Journey

Growing,Bloom by Bloom

The Beginning

A passion becomes a business

Founded in Dome with small church events and family parties, building a reputation one grateful client at a time.

Growth Years

Weddings become our signature

Word spread. Full wedding transformations — traditional and white — became our most requested service, and our floral studio was born.

Expansion

Rentals & balloon artistry join the family

We invested in our own Chiavari chairs, linens, backdrops and lighting — so clients get one team, one truck, one standard.

Today

500+ events and counting

From intimate naming ceremonies to corporate galas, the Shekinah signature now graces celebrations all across Greater Accra.
